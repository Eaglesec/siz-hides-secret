FAQs

What is Digi-Cloak?

Digi-Cloak is online web tool to hide secret text in images using crytography and steganography.

How does it work?

Plain text is encrypted into cypher text using AES with a 256-bit key. This key is derived from a password specified by you or generated using our secure password generator in your browser. Encrypted cypher text is hidden in the image using LSB (Least Significant Bit) technique in such a way that the image is unaffected. The image is also provided by you or a sample image can be used.

What information is retained?

No information is retained, all the process happens client-side only.

How secure is my content?

Your plain text, password and image are never transmitted, all functionality takes place client-side in your browser.

I misplaced my password, is my content recoverable?

No, that is the point. Your plain text, password and image are never submitted beyond your browser. If the password or image is misplaced, the original content is gone forever.

Is there a limit to the amount of content?

There is no limit to the amount, but the increasing length of plain text decreases the quality of the output image.
